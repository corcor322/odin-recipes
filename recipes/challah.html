Banana Chocolate Chip Challah

Description:

Sweet tooth? Try this 'twist' on a classic braided challah.

Ingredients:

  • 5 c all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 4 1/2 tsp instant yeast
  • 2 very ripe medium bananas, mashed
  • 1 c warm water
  • 1/2 c olive oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 c semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 c flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add water, olive oil, eggs, and mashed bananas, sitrring until smooth.
  3. Add remaining flower 1/2 c at a time, until flour is moist but no longer sticky. Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Transfer dough to floured surface and knead until smooth.
  5. Place ball of dough into oiled bowl. Cover and let rise for 30 minutes or until doubled in size.
  6. Divide dough into three equal strands. Pinch each end and braid the strands together. Cover additional 20 minutes to rise while oven is preheating to 375 degrees F.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden brown. Let cool on baking tray. Store leftovers in airtight container at room temperature.
