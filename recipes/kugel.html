Kugel

Description:

A tasty dish of noodles with a hint of sweetness and cinnamon. Perfect for shabbat or any occation!

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz wide egg noodles
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 lb sour cream
  • 8 oz cottage cheese
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 c sugar
  • 1/4 c unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Cinnamon and sugar for dusting
  • Nonstick cooking oil spray

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add noodles to the pot, and cook until tender. Drain and return noodles to the pot.
  3. Blend together eggs, sour cream, cream cheese, sugar, melted butter, and salt.
  4. Pour the mixture over the pot of cooked noodles and stir until well combined
  5. Spray a 9x13 in baking dish with nonstick cooking oil. Pour the noodle mixture into the dish and top with cinnamon and sugar.
  6. Bake for 60 minutes or until tops of noodles are golden brown. Remove from oven and rest 15-20 minutes before slicing. Serve warm or cold.
